How Help to Care works as an Organisation?

Get in contact with our Team

As an organisation wanting to make use of the Help to Care Service please contact us and we'll require proof and generate accounts for the professional caregivers

 Log in with your account

An account is generated for your staff. The staff can then search by location and a contact email/phone number will be displayed for the closest volunteer.

 The volunteer provides assistance

The Volunteer will then follow a set list of curated instructions based on the specific assistance required
