How Help to Health works as a Volunteer?

Register as a Volunteer

As an untrained volunteer you register on the website and provide your Name, Email, Phone and Location. You can do that here!

 Training

Volunteers can be trained to give the care they are capable and comfortable providing to their local community. Approved training resources are supplied online, including signposts to help link volunteers together, and with other local volunteer organisations. Volunteers can help the community and retain the flexibility they need to fulfil personal commitments without affecting their own health and well-being

 You're contacted to take action

You will be contacted VIA email/phone based on your proximity and availabiliy to the person who requires help. You will be guided though the caregiving process with assistance available should you require it.
