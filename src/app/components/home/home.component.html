Welcome to Help to Health!

HelpToHealth is an online platform for Care Organisations to rapidly connect and educate local volunteers to provide care in their community.

Features & Benefits:

  • The community seeks care from local Care Organisations who they already know and trust
  • Care Organisations can quickly build and train a network of local care volunteers that can be rapidly deployed to meet the care needs of the local community
    • Care needs of the community can be quickly provided
    • A network of local caregivers can be quickly established and trained to meet basic care needs in the community
    • While meeting immediate care needs, the volunteers can provide practical, medically-approved, self-care advice to the care-seeker
  • Caregiving Volunteers can register their details, and sign up to receive online training for the care they want to provide to their local area, at the times they are available
    • Volunteers can be trained to give the care they are capable and comfortable providing to their local community
    • Approved training resources are supplied online, including signposts to help link volunteers together, and with other local volunteer organisations
    • Volunteers can help the community and retain the flexibility they need to fulfil personal commitments without affecting their own health and well-being
    • Volunteers with existing care expertise can register to offer that care to others in their community
